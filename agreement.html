Scope of Service

A research analyst studies companies and industries, analyses raw data, and makes forecasts or recommendations about whether to buy, hold or sell securities. Niiveshneeti analyses information to provide recommendations about investments in securities to the clients.

Research Analyst & his team may assist clients in understanding the research / trade & execution. But the client will be solely and in totality responsible for all his trades done. He will be in full capacity responsible for his capital risk, quantity management & trade Profit/losses. Neither Research Analyst nor his team can be held liable for any profits or losses or his capital risk directly or indirectly at any point of time.

Risk Disclosure On Derivatives — SEBI Study

  • 9 out of 10 individual traders in equity Futures and Options Segment incurred net losses.
  • On an average, loss makers registered net trading loss close to ₹50,000.
  • Over and above the net trading losses incurred, loss makers expended an additional 28% of the net trading losses as transaction costs.
  • Those making net trading profits incurred between 15% to 50% of such profits as transaction costs.
