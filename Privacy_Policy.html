Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how IndexNeeti collects, uses, protects, and manages information shared through its website and related communication channels.

Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is important to us, and we are dedicated to safeguarding it. To ensure transparency in how we collect, use, and manage your information, we have outlined our online data practices and the choices available to you in this notice.

Information We Collect

We collect certain details when you voluntarily share them through our website or social media platforms. The purpose of gathering this information is to enhance our services and deliver the best possible experience for you.

Data Accuracy and Protection

We make every effort to keep your information accurate and secure. Maintaining the confidentiality and protection of your data is our top priority.

We implement security measures to safeguard any personal details you provide. However, while we strive to keep your information safe, we cannot guarantee complete security.

Any data you transmit to us via our online services is at your own discretion and risk.

Service and Promotional Communication

Please be aware that you may receive promotional calls, contest invitations, or notifications regarding service-related activities.

Beyond our core services, we may also use your contact details, including your mobile number and email address, to share newsletters, surveys, contest details, or updates on new services that may be beneficial to you.

By subscribing to our services, you acknowledge and agree to receive such communications from IndexNeeti.

Sharing of Information

Your personal information, whether public or private, will remain with us. However, it may be shared with regulatory authorities, affiliated organizations, or relevant third parties where necessary, without requiring additional consent.

Inquiry Form and Contact Consent

By submitting the Inquiry Form on our website or reaching out to us through any other platform, including social media, you consent to being contacted via phone call, SMS, or WhatsApp on the number you provide.

Even if your number is registered under the National Do Not Disturb (DND) registry, you agree to receive messages and calls from us.

Respect for Your Trust

We value your trust and assure you that your privacy will always be treated with the utmost respect.
