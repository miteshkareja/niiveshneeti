Resolution timeline

We aim to resolve all grievances within 21 working days from the date of receipt.

Escalation to SCORES

If your grievance is not resolved within the above timeframe, you can escalate it to SEBI’s SCORES Platform, which is the SEBI Complaint Redressal System.

SCORES Portal: scores.sebi.gov.in

Online Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with the resolution provided through our support channel or through the SCORES platform, you may access the Online Dispute Resolution portal.

ODR Portal: smartodr.in

Important note

Please use the official support and escalation channels only, and keep your complaint details, supporting documents, and communication records ready for faster resolution.
